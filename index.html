// 001 — The Concept

A Protest In Code

$WRONG tracks the US–Iran military conflict in real time. Verified war events trigger community-voted token burns — removing $WRONG from circulation permanently, on-chain, forever.

The mechanics are simple: escalation drives the burn rate. A single airstrike burns 10,000 tokens. A civilian death burns 1,000,000. The supply shrinks as the conflict grows. The coin becomes a living ledger of destruction.
