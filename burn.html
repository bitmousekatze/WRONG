// Questions

Burn FAQ

Can I recover tokens I've burned?

+

No. Burns are permanent and irreversible. Tokens sent to the burn address are gone forever. This is the point — the destruction is real, on-chain, and permanent.

What if a burn target isn't fully filled?

+

Partial burns count. The ledger records whatever was burned toward each event. Events stay open until filled or until a new event supersedes them. There's no deadline — the ledger is permanent.

How do I know my burn was counted?

+

Post your tx hash in Discord. The bot verifies it on Solscan. You can also verify independently — every transaction to the burn address is publicly visible on Solscan. The ledger is updated manually by admins based on verified Discord submissions.

Can I burn for multiple events at once?

+

Yes — you can split a burn across multiple events by posting multiple tx hashes and specifying which event each is for. Or burn in one transaction and assign the full amount to one event.

Is there a minimum burn amount?

+

No minimum. 1 token to 1 billion — all burns are counted. The ledger records every contribution regardless of size.
