How It Works

Six steps from verified war event to permanent on-chain burn. No shortcuts. No unverified claims. Every burn is sourced, voted, reviewed, and publicly recorded.

// The Burn Process
01
Event Detected
A community member spots a verified war event from an official source — US Department of Defense, UN OCHA, UN Human Rights (OHCHR), or IAEA. No social media. No unverified news outlets. Only official government and international organization reports.
02
Submission
The event and its official source link are posted in the Discord #burn-submissions channel. The submission must include the event type, date, location, and a direct link to the official source document.
03
Community Vote
The community votes on the submission. A simple majority is required to confirm the event. This prevents any single actor from manipulating the burn ledger. Every holder has a voice.
04
Admin Review
After the community vote passes, an admin independently verifies the source. The admin has final say and can reject any submission that doesn't meet the sourcing standard — even if the vote passed. Integrity over speed.
05
On-Chain Burn
The admin executes the burn transaction on Solana. The tokens are sent to a verified, public burn wallet — removing them from circulation permanently. The transaction hash is recorded.
06
Ledger Update
The burn is recorded on the public burn ledger with the Solana transaction hash, the source link, the event type, date, and the exact number of tokens burned. Anyone can verify any burn at any time on Solscan.

// Questions

FAQ

Is $WRONG an investment?

+

No. $WRONG is a meme coin and a protest mechanism. It is not financial advice. Do not buy $WRONG expecting financial returns. The value proposition is the statement, not the speculation.

What happens when the war ends?

+

Burns stop. The ledger remains. The final burn count becomes a permanent, immutable, on-chain record of the conflict's human and material cost. The remaining supply is whatever survived the war.

Can burns be faked or manipulated?

+

Every burn requires an official source (DoD, UN, IAEA), a community majority vote, and independent admin verification. All burns are on-chain with public transaction hashes. The system is designed to be transparent and verifiable at every step.

Why Solana and Pump.fun?

+

Solana offers fast, low-cost transactions ideal for frequent burns. Pump.fun enables a fair launch with no team allocation, no presale, and no insider advantages. The bonding curve ensures transparent price discovery.

Is there a team allocation?

+

Zero. $WRONG launched with 0% team allocation on Pump.fun's fair launch platform. No insiders, no vesting, no special wallets. The entire supply was available to the public from the start.

Will $WRONG track other conflicts?

+

Phase 1 is focused exclusively on the US–Iran conflict. Future phases may expand scope, but the immediate priority is maintaining the highest possible sourcing and verification standard for one conflict before considering expansion.
